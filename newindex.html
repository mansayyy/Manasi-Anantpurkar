RESEARCH

CCRNET

Customer Conversion Rate Network

Background

Customers are of paramount importance to a company. Store owners need to analyze and comprehend the customer experience when they visit a shop. Based on the customer's emotional and behavioral responses, diverse products are suggested to the customer. A vital part of this project involves computing an interest score by establishing a process for detecting customer emotions, analyzing their poses, and estimating nonverbal cues using neural networks, deep learning, and computer vision. This deep learning based implementation of the defined logical model is found to return reasonable estimations of a customer's interest states. These estimations are highly correlated with visual-cue-based interest states that a customer may exist in.
